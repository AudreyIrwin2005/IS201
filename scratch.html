Audrey's Workout Schedule

Resume Bottle Flip

Alternating A-Days & B-Days

  1. A-Day Schedule: Start with a 10-20 minute run @ any pace followed by a 10-15 minute walk
    • 20 Weighed Squats
    • 15 Weighted Lunges
    • 15 Weighted Deadbugs
    • 15 Weighted Raised Russian Twists
    • 1-Minute Plank
    • Repeat 2 more times replacing squats with hip dips during the 3rd set.


  2. B-Day Schedule: Start with a 10-20 minute run @ any pace followed by a 10-15 minute walk
    • 20 Bicycle crunches
    • 15 Leg Raises
    • 20 Weighted Raised Russian Twists
    • 1-Minute Plank
    • 15 Hip Dips
    • Repeat 2 more times
